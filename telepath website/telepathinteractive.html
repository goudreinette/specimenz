The World's Most Lifelike Virtual Pet!

Pioneering the Future of Play with our My-Core Technology

This spring, TelePath has unveiled its most ambitious project to date: Brainletz. This cutting-edge game will be the first to make use of a groundbreaking innovation in the field of integrated circuit design: our MyCore processor chip.

The MyCore processor represents a fundamental shift in how we understand interactive entertainment. Unlike static, inorganic hardware, the MyCore’s live component learns, reads and responds to user input. It adapts to the player’s problem-solving patterns and developmental pace, allowing the game, its locations and its characters to evolve into a unique environment, personalized for every user. The bond formed with a My-Core game is permanent.

TelePath Interactive is dedicated to the creation of intelligent and engaging interactive software for young learners. We believe the greatest adventures lie within. Our latest games, powered by groundbreaking bio-technology, allow for an unprecedented relationship between player and game.

The World’s Most Lifelike Virtual Pet

Brainletz takes your child on a journey through the mind, inviting them to explore what makes every living being unique through taking care of an intelligent creature: the Brainlet.

The Brainlet is a blank slate, a nascent, playful intelligence that learns, adapts and evolves through interaction with your child. By guiding their Brainletz through immersive environments, solving puzzles together and engaging in reflective activities, children are encouraged to teach, train and evolve their Brainletz. Your child will watch in wonder as their Brainlet develops into a creature with an almost human-like level of intelligence, as you in turn will watch your child develop a deeper understanding of themselves.

Toys to Life

For a disk-based game to make use of the power of the MyCore chip, we designed a proprietary console called the Brainletz ‘Pod’, which acts as the in-universe housing for the Brainlet, while also providing a housing for the MyCore chip. When connected to a PC, the Pod will grant access to the core gameplay, but it can also be carried around and played with on the go as a keychain-style virtual pet. Any data gained by the Pod during on-the-go play gets transferred to the main game on linkage with a PC, providing real benefits to regular on-the-go training.

The My-Connect function allows the Pod unit to connect to the TelePath servers for data transfer and synchronization. This will allow users to link up and befriend other players’ Brainletz, visit their Pod’s, and even communicate through a safe-chat function.
